Manaoag

Manaoag began as a small settlement called Cawili, located by Baloquing Creek, in the early 1600s. The Augustinian Fathers established a Christian mission there, known as the Mission of Sta. Monica. The Dominican Fathers, who succeeded the Augustinians and were devout followers of the Blessed Virgin Mary, placed the settlements under the protection of the "Queen of the Most Holy Rosary."
