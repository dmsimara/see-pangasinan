Anda

Anda was formally occupied in the year 1842, only becoming an official town back on May 10, 1859. It was previously an uninhabited area with stopovers for fishermen and seafarers. The town was established when Don Domingo, Don Sixto, and Don Gregorio Cacho Valerio invited people from neighboring provinces to settle in Anda.
