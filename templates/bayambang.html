Bayambang

Bayambang was once a much larger town encompassing other municipalities like Bautista, Alcala, Sto. Tomas, and Rosales. It was founded in the 16th Century by an Aeta known as Agalet. Legend has it that the town's name originated from a part of a plant called "Balangbang".
