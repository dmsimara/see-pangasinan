Bautista

Bautista achieved municipal status in 1900. In 1899, Jose Palma wrote the poem "Filipinas" in this town, which later became the lyrics of the Philippine National Anthem. Bautista is also renowned for its "walis tambo," earning it the nickname "The Walis Tambo Capital of Pangasinan."
