Agno

Agno is currently (hugely) known for the Balingasay River and Umbrella Rocks, which are located to the west of Pangasinan. In historical context, it is referred to as the Old Seat of Civilization as it was the first town ever to produce a primary school (1902) and an intermediate school (1906), neighboring towns (of their populations) of Bani, Bolinao, Dasol, Infanta, and Mabini have turned their eyes to Agno for educational purposes.
