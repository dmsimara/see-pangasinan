Mabini

Formerly named “Balincaguig” or the “Home of Bats”, Mabini was originally a part of Zambales. It existed around 1800 under Don Isidro Puzon. Its first site, the Conventa, was the initial settlement until the local population had to evacuate due to a great flood in 1832.
