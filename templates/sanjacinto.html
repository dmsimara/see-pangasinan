San Jacinto

San Jacinto was established on August 17, 1598, by Rev. Fr. Herminigildo Milgar and named after Father Jacinto, who was canonized by Pope Clemente in 1598. San Jacinto became a regular municipality in 1601, making it one of the oldest municipalities in the province.
