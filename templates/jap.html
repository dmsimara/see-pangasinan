Japanese Occupation

December 22, 1941: Japanese forces invade Pangasinan during World War II.
January 9-13, 1945: Allied Forces land in Lingayen and begin the liberation of Pangasinan.
February 1945: The provincial government is restored with Sofronio Quimson as governor and Dagupan temporarily as the capital.
