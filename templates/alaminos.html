Alaminos City

Namely famous for the world-renowned Hundred Islands it has. Previously a part of Bolinao (one of the nine northernmost towns of Zambales), it became independent of its mother town in the year 1747. The name itself was to honor Lieutenant (Governor) General Alaminos (1872).
