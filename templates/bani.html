Bani

Formerly known as San Simon, Bani was once part of Northern Zambales. It was annexed to the province of Pangasinan on November 21, 1903. The town's name originated from an incident involving the disappearance and reappearance of the Immaculate Concepcion statue under a "Bani" tree during a town fiesta.
