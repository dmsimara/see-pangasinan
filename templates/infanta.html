Infanta

Formerly a barrio of Sta. Cruz, Zambales, Infanta only became a municipality by virtue of a Superior Decree issued by the Spanish government on October 4, 1876. The town is referred to as “Infanta”, meaning ‘young’, as it was the offspring of Sta. Cruz.
