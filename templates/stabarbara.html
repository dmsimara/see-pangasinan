Santa Barbara

Santa Barbara, formerly known as "Sta. Barbara de Tolong", was founded on October 30, 1741. It is recognized for its cultural heritage and was the hometown of Don Daniel B. Maramba, known as the "Grand Old Man of Pangasinan". During his term as the 7th Governor of Pangasinan, the Provincial Capitol Building was constructed in 1918.
